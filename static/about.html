Our Story

Who We Are &
Why We Exist

CostBid Solutions was founded on a simple belief — that supply chain firms deserve better than guesswork. We bring precision, data, and domain expertise to every bid and cost decision.

Our Background

Built by Supply Chain
Practitioners

CostBid Solutions was founded by supply chain professionals who spent years inside manufacturing, logistics, and procurement organisations — watching companies lose contracts because of poorly structured bids or bleed margin because of uncontrolled costs.

We built CostBid to solve exactly that problem. Our team combines deep domain knowledge with financial rigour, delivering bid and costing services that are grounded in how supply chains actually work — not how textbooks say they do.

Today we serve clients across manufacturing, logistics, automotive, and IoT sectors, helping them win more contracts and protect their margins on every project.
