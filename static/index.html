What We Offer

Bidding Services

We prepare technically sound, competitively priced bids that maximize your win rate without sacrificing your margins.

  • RFQ / RFP response preparation
  • Competitive bid pricing strategy
  • Vendor qualification & evaluation
  • Tender document review & analysis

02

Costing Services

Detailed, accurate cost modeling across your entire supply chain — from raw materials to last-mile delivery.

  • Bill of Materials (BOM) costing
  • Landed cost computation
  • Overhead & logistics cost allocation
  • Should-cost modeling
  • Multi-tier supplier cost analysis

  • Value engineering recommendations
  • Monthly cost performance reporting
