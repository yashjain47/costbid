Automotive · Cost Optimization
Reducing Procurement Costs by ₹1.8 Crore for a Tier-1 Auto Parts Supplier
The Challenge

A Tier-1 automotive supplier was experiencing margin erosion across three product lines due to rising raw material costs and uncompetitive supplier contracts. They needed a full cost audit and renegotiation strategy within 60 days before their annual contract review cycle.

Our Approach

We conducted a full BOM audit across all three product lines, benchmarked supplier pricing against live market rates, and identified 14 specific procurement leakages. We then developed a structured renegotiation playbook for their procurement team.
